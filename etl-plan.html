Rhino Federated Computing

Harmonizing Real-World Oncology Data to the GENIE Data Model

Rhino Health's Data Harmonization Engine (DHE) was used to transform the publicly accessible AACR GENIE BPC BrCa v1.0-public dataset — a multimodal real-world oncology dataset spanning genomic, clinical, and treatment records — into the standardized GENIE Data Model (GDM v1.0.1), enabling federated analysis across institutions without moving patient data.

AACR Project GENIE®
Powering Precision Medicine
The Challenge

Precision medicine requires an end-to-end learning health care system, wherein treatment decisions for patients are informed by the prior experiences of similar patients. A major challenge is that no single institution is able to sequence and treat sufficient numbers of patients to improve clinical-decision making independently. To overcome this, AACR launched Project GENIE.

The Registry

AACR Project GENIE® is a publicly accessible cancer registry of real-world clinico-genomic data assembled through data sharing between 20 leading international cancer centers. Through strategic partners Sage Bionetworks and cBioPortal, the registry aggregates, harmonizes, and links clinical-grade next-generation cancer genomic sequencing data with clinical outcomes obtained during routine medical practice.

The Impact

The consortium is driven by openness, transparency, and inclusion — ensuring that project output remains accessible to the global cancer research community for the benefit of all. AACR Project GENIE provides another knowledge turn in the virtuous cycle of research, accelerating the pace of drug discovery, improving clinical trial design, and ultimately benefiting cancer patients globally.
