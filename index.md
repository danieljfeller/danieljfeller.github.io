  <h2>About</h2>

  <p>I'm a healthcare data science leader with 10+ years in health informatics and machine learning. I design federated computing infrastructure that lets healthcare and other regulated industries collaborate on sensitive data without ever moving it. At Rhino Health, I've led the technical design of federated computing projects with major biopharma companies and built real-world data networks spanning dozens of clinical sites.</p>

  <p>I've authored 30+ peer-reviewed studies on clinical NLP, HIV research, and health informatics, and I'm the author of <a href="/book/">Decoding Health Data</a>, a practical guide to healthcare data systems. I'm an expert in common data models including HL7 FHIR and OMOP, and I build scalable AI and data infrastructure with AWS, Azure, Python, PySpark, and SQL.</p>

  <p>I completed my PhD at Columbia University researching machine learning and human-computer interaction in clinical settings. I'm always open to select advisory and consulting conversations at the intersection of healthcare, data, and AI.</p>
